About AIET:
Alva's Institute of Engineering and Technology is located in Mijar, Moodubidri, Dakshina Kannada, Karnataka.
It was established in 2008 by Dr. M. Mohan Alva under Alva's Education Foundation.
It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.
NAAC accredited institution. CET Code: E179.
