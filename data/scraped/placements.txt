AIET Campus Placements and Training:

The Training and Placement Cell at AIET bridges academic learning with professional employment.
It provides training in soft skills, technical knowledge, and interview preparation through workshops, seminars, and boot camps.

Campus Selection Statistics:
- 218 companies participated in campus placements
- 406 students placed
- 521 multiple offers received

Annual Mega Placement Drive - Pragati:
AIET organizes "Pragati", a mega placement drive every year.
The 16th edition will be held in August 2026.
Over 300 companies and thousands of candidates participate.
Previous Pragati statistics: 272 companies, 18316 students participated, 4903 shortlisted, 622 spot offers.

100% Placement Record:
The 2019 placement drive achieved 100% placement. All participating students received offers within 3 days.
