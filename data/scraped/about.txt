AIET Overview:

Alva's Institute of Engineering and Technology, also called AIET, is located in Mijar, Moodubidri, Karnataka.

AIET was founded in 2008 under the leadership of Dr. M. Mohan Alva.

The institute is part of Alva's Education Foundation.

AIET aims to provide affordable quality professional education and develop engineers, leaders, and innovators.

Vision:
To be recognized as a center of excellence in engineering education, innovation, and research, producing globally competent professionals committed to societal development.

Mission:
- Deliver value-based technical education with focus on innovation and sustainability.
- Nurture professional ethics, leadership qualities, and entrepreneurial spirit.
- Promote research, innovation, and industry collaboration for real-world challenges.
- Ensure holistic development through cultural, social, and environmental awareness.

Academic Focus:
AIET offers undergraduate engineering programs and a postgraduate MBA program.

Campus Strengths:
- Academic excellence
- Research and innovation
- Industry collaboration
- Holistic student development
- Cultural and sports activities
- Student clubs and forums
